The color of the year 2024 is peach, which is a light semi-saturated color in between orange and pink. It is vibrant yet muted enough to blend into the group of neutral colors. I will be wearing a mix of orange, red, and pink this season as my accent colors. When I want to be a bit more muted, or when I do not have items in these colors, I will use tan or brown as accents instead. My base colors will be cream white, navy blue, and black, which will help pair well with my accent colors and balance them out. I hope to get good wear out of my sparkly necklaces and my cute bracelets, as well as get some good use out of the few belts I own. I challenge myself to create looks that incorporate butterflies, flowers, and colors in the orange-red group. A detailed neckline can be created with a detailed collared top, light scarf, or necklace, an accent belt, accent bracelet, and/or a bottom with small details on the hem. The silhouette I will try to wear more often is the accentuated waist.
