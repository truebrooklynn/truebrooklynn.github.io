Eating in the Works

| August 03 2022 |

I am a firm believer of "you are what you eat", "calories in, calories out" and that eating street food / eating out in general is neither healthy nor sustainable. I've lost count of the number of times I spent money on food or eating out, only to not like what I ordered. May I emphasize that these are my BELIEFS for my habits. How I recommend finding your own values and preferences in food -- your style, if you will, then sticking to it.

Drinks

Yes: water, milk (cow, almond soy)
Trying not to drink: soda, juice
Will not drink: coconut milk, alcohol, coffee.
As for tea, well.. I like: green tea, red tea, fruity/herbal tea. I don't like: earl grey. It smells nice but tastes not-so-nice.

Meats

Any except for turkey. Not a fan of turkey.

Dairy

Any.

Vegetables/Fruits

Anything but coconut. Actually..is coconut a fruit?

X candy X deep fried foods, heavily oiled foods X fries / chips / icecream* X BBQ*
