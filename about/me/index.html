About Me

Welcome! I'm Brooke, a creative soul navigating life in my twenties. Currently, I'm working on a sustainable and cohesive wardrobe of practices where everything in life fits seamlessly together and each and every one receives dedicated use and love.

More About My Journey

I strive to curate a timeless collection while minimizing purchases. My interest started in a chatter-filled room, sketching cartoon characters. This transitioned to re-creating pieces to wear and decorate my space, then grew to reusing, repurposing, and restyling items for customization and self-expression.

To describe my style in short: clean, cute, and cozy. I bring enthusiasm to changing seasons and trends. I am fun-loving, creative, and open-minded. I am comfortable being my own self that I can refuse trends I don't like and self-conscious to ask others if I am dressed right for the occasion.

In my free time, I enjoy walks in the outdoors. The world around me inspires to write prose and poetry. I also love to draw, make crafts, and paint. My favorite subjects of art are animals and people.

I believe style is expressed in all aspects of life: dress, habits, living spaces, and more.

This website a place for journaling my process of building my style, personal introspection, and potentially inspiring others to find their own style.

Lots of hugs,
