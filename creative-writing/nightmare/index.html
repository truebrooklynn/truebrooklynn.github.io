Nightmare

Written by Brooklynne Toff. August 8, 2022.

A short poem.

This work could have adult content or themes. If you continue reading, you have agreed that you are willing to see such content and themes.

Nightmare

Sticky clumps on the tip of my eyelids,

There are trees and harsh gasps—

Up and down

Up and down

Legs and fingers, they won’t

Move.


The sky is the ceiling,

The trees are the cream walls,

Something soft is beneath me, and

Bed sheets are where the soil used to be.


Where am I?


Eyes slowly correcting,

Ceiling fan not whirling,

Fingers brush soft pajamas,

A moment of clarity,


Ah, I see,


We are back here again.
