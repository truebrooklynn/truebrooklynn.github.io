The Third Listener

Written by Brooklynne Toff. August 8, 2022.

A short poem about being the third listener during a quarrel.

This work could have adult content or themes. If you continue reading, you have agreed that you are willing to see such content and themes.

The Third Listener

Stop, stop, sh, sh.

Do not move,/p>

Do not speak.

Stay put, in the dark.

Count, count, breathe, breath.

It doesn’t matter, don’t you see?


This is how it is meant to be.
