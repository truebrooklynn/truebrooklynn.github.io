Brooke's Favorite Trends for Summer 2022

It's Summer 2022

Written by Brooklynn Toffe

March 31, 2022

Detailed top and simple bottom.

There are solid, two-tone colors. One saturated, one light. Light colored clothing with darker, small details. Statement collar or halter top. A black long blazer vest paired with a casual t-shirt. A solid color with a large statement print or design. Clothing with sleek and structured arms. I wonder how that will work and not be hot.
